Fireside Chat Response

10/12/2014

Shereef's Fireside Chat definitely confirmed many of the reasons I was drawn to and joined DBC. I really enjoy learning when I'm surrounded by a relatively small group of like-minded people where there's a strong sense of camaraderie. I try to look for this sense of community whenever joining any sort of program or institution. So far everyone I have met that is involved with DBC has been great to work with. Although I am understandably nervous to take this next step in my life, I am very excited for it.

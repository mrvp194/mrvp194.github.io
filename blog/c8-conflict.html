Thoughts From My Head

Conflict

11/30/2014

Recently I was on a 45 day backpacking trip in the Australian wild. When you're around the same group of people every single hour for 45 days without much alone time it's easy to get sick of some of those people around you. Torwards the in of the trip I found myself being passive aggressive to some of the others. Ultimately, my actions made the situation worse, and I ended up leaving somewhat dissapointed. Looking back on it I defenitely wish I could have been more accepting of their flaws. I have read that often what you find most annoying about someone else is something you don't like about yourself. I will try to keep this in mind as I go forward.

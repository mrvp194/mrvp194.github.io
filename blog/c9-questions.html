Asking Good Questions

12/07/2014

Asking fairly simple questions to Google has worked out fairly well so far. Usually there is a similar question on stackoverflow that helps me. Documentation can also be helpful. However, there have been a couple times where my questions were slightly more specific and Google just could not come up with an answer for me, or maybe the resources provided weren't that descriptive. I can think of specifically the bingo challenge we had to complete. I found to very hard to even come up with the right query for Google. Luckily, in these cases I've been able to figure it out on my own after taking a break from work for a bit. I've really grown to appreciate stackoverflow over past weeks, it's been a great help.
